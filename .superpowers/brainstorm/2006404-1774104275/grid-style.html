Polar Grid Style

What kind of polar grid overlay are you envisioning?

A. Rings Only

Concentric distance rings with labels. Clean and minimal — shows range at a glance without visual clutter.

B. Full Polar Grid

Range rings + azimuth lines (N/S/E/W and diagonals) with cardinal direction and distance labels. Classic radar-style overlay.

C. Full Sector Grid

Range rings + 30-degree sector lines with degree labels. Maximum precision for bearing analysis — closer to the screenshot in the issue.
